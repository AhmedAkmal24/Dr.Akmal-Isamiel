About Dr. Akmal نبذة عن د. أكمل

Dr. Akmal Esmaiel Mohamed is a distinguished legal professional with over 30 years of experience in providing comprehensive legal services. Having completed his Master's and Doctoral studies in law, Dr. Akmal combines extensive practical experience with advanced legal knowledge and academic excellence.
